Untitled (Foxes) Performance Video

Through the Hallways of Shatterproof Glass Out Now

"Live" performance of Untitled (Foxes) recorded at my home studio back in Portland. I edited the track so I could play pretty much all of it from my MIDI controller through looping and automating effects and volume. After that, I just recorded the vocals over the track and added a few things in post to keep it interesting. So, not quite live but at least it looks pretty cool.
